Battleship Trivia is a new spin on an old classic. This game combines classic Battleship with Trivia. Hit ‘Start New Game’ to begin a match. When the match begins, players will take turns attempting to answer trivia questions and sink their opponent's battleships. At the start of each turn, the player will be presented with a trivia question. If you answer the question correctly, you will be allowed to guess a location on your opponent's board. If you answer incorrectly, your turn is over and the other player will begin their turn. The game ends when one player has sunk all of their opponents ships. You may start a new game at any time by hitting ‘New Game’ in the top navigation bar. The game will be abandoned as long as both players agree the game should end.
